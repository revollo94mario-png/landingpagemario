# Portfolio Landing Page Brief

## Direction
Keep the page short, quiet, and confident.

The goal is not to explain everything. The goal is to make your work legible in a few seconds:

- what you do
- how broad your range is
- where people should click next

The page should feel editorial, minimal, and deliberate.

## Recommended Structure

### 1. Hero
One strong headline, one supporting paragraph, two actions.

Suggested layout:

- Small top line: `Portfolio`
- Main heading
- Short supporting text
- Two buttons: `View work` and `Contact`

## Layout Notes

- Keep everything inside a restrained container width.
- Use generous whitespace above and below the hero.
- Let typography do most of the work.
- Avoid long paragraphs.
- Keep card descriptions to 1 or 2 sentences maximum.
- smooth scrolling and transitions
- Framer-like professional animations
- responsive On mobile, stack the cards in this order:
  - Photography
  - Content
  - Apps
  - Websites
  - Contact
